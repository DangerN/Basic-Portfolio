About Me

Hello! I'm just a 22 year old Madisonian foodie who loves to explore different cultures through their dishes. In my free time, I like to play videogames on my PS4 or Nintendo Switch, embroider, meditate, go on hikes, take care of my plant babies, shopping and just having a good time with good company! I am currently a graphic designer/pre-flight specialist for the Department of Admin - Publishing and Distribution Center. Graphic designing has been a catalyst for my interest in coding. I found that I enjoy not only the designing aspect of CSS, but also highly interested in the magic behind completing a webpage using HTML and JavaScript.
